**FAQ: Vultr New Customer Eligibility**

**Q: Can I create a new Vultr account if I already have one, just to get the promo?**
A: No. Vultr explicitly states duplicate accounts are not eligible for any promotions. Creating a second account to claim credits violates their Terms of Service and can result in account suspension.

**Q: Do I need to make a deposit to claim the free credit?**
A: With a credit card, you can link it with a $0 deposit. For other payment methods (PayPal, crypto, Alipay), a small deposit is required before credits activate. Some new accounts in certain regions may also be asked for an additional deposit as a fraud check — this appears to be case-by-case.

**Q: Does the promotional credit expire?**
A: Yes. Standard promo codes (FLYTWOHUNDRED, 250VULTRFLY, referral credits) expire 30 days after issuance. The VULTRMATCH bonus credit expires 12 months after issuance. Unused credit disappears — no rollover, no refund.

**Q: Can I use the free promo credit on GPU instances?**
A: No. GPU products (NVIDIA GH200, H100, HGX A100) are excluded from promotional credit usage. Trial credit covers standard Cloud Compute and most Optimized Cloud Compute products.

**Q: What happens if my promo credit runs out before 30 days?**
A: Vultr will charge your linked payment method for any usage beyond your credit balance. Monitor your usage in the billing section — Vultr's pricing is hourly, so you won't be surprised by a sudden monthly bill.

**Q: Can I combine multiple promo codes?**
A: No. Vultr's terms state promotional credits cannot be combined with any other offers. Pick the highest-value code available and apply that one.

**Q: Is the Free Tier Program the same as the promo credit?**
A: No. The Free Tier Program is a separate, application-based program offering a permanently free small instance (1 vCPU / 512 MB RAM). Promo credits are temporary trial funds. You can apply for both independently.
